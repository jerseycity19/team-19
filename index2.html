Our Purpose

The inability to see other people’s humanity leads to world chaos. A continuously divided world leads to no empathy and compassion. Our mission is to encourage curiosity in young people to see past assumptions. Some will want to practice their English, others just want to share and be heard without judgement. In the end, we may look different and have different life experiences, but we are all just human underneath.

Global Nomads Mission

Global Nomads Group (GNG) is committed to fostering dialogue and understanding among the world’s youth. Bridging culture gaps and shattering misconceptions, they work at the intersection of education, cultural exchange, and conflict resolution with the goal of equipping the next generation of young people with the skills and knowledge they need to thrive in our increasingly connected and complex world.

CURIOSITY | VULNERABILITY

Curiosity allows you to experience the world in new and unexpected ways. Explore your own assumptions and ideas by asking generous questions and embracing the responses.

You are encouraged to come as you are and welcome others to do the same during every conversation. A safe space must be created collectively to allow everyone to be their authentic selves

RESPECT | DREAMS & IDEAS

Everyone's stories, opinions, and feelings matter. Assume the best in each other by acknowledging our shared humanity. See the good in others and they will see it in you.

Sharing dreams and ideas with people from around the world - and hearing theirs - expands our potential. Let's see what we might learn about ourselves and our world by taking a seat at the table.
